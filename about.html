Team Appetit
Appétit Inc. is an Indian food delivering marketplace company which aims at providing good quality food to students, employees and old age people who cannot cook. It was founded in August 2017 at the heart of Kolkata. We are a team of enthusiastic CS Engineers who seek to make a difference in the way of life taking one problem at a time. The company is evolving and we'd like your support and suggestions.
